Admission

Join Brazendragonj Ram Chandra High School — Academic Year 2026–27

How to Apply

1

Check Eligibility

Student must have passed Class V (PSC) for admission to Class VI. Age limit: 10–13 years.

2

Collect Application Form

Collect the admission form from school office or fill the online form below. Cost: ৳100.

3

Submit Documents

Submit the filled form along with required documents to the school office.

4

Admission Test

Attend the written admission test. Subjects: Bengali, English, Mathematics.

5

Result & Enrollment

Results published within 3 days. Selected students complete enrollment with fee payment.

Required Documents

PSC / Junior Certificate (original & copy)
Birth Certificate (original & copy)
4 copies passport size photographs
Father's NID card copy
Testimonial from previous school
Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school
