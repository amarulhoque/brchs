About Our School

Brazendragonj Ram Chandra High School, established in 1938, stands as one of the oldest and most respected secondary educational institutions in Derai, Sunamganj.

We offer quality education from Class VI to Class X, preparing students for the SSC examination under the Bangladesh Board with a strong focus on academic excellence and moral development.
