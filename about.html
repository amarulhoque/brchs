Our History

Brazendragonj Ram Chandra High School was founded in 1938 by a group of visionary community leaders who believed in the power of education to transform lives in the Derai region of Sunamganj.

For over 85 years, we have been a beacon of learning in the haor belt of Sunamganj district, producing thousands of graduates who have gone on to serve in every field of national life — from civil service and medicine to education and business.

Today, the school proudly serves over 800 students from Class VI to Class X, maintaining its founding commitment to quality education, moral character, and community development.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide quality secondary education that develops academic excellence, strong moral values, and a love of learning in every student from Classes VI to X.

🔭 Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Sunamganj district, empowering students to become responsible, educated citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.

💡 Our Values

Integrity, discipline, respect, and academic excellence guide everything we do. We believe every student has unique potential waiting to be discovered.
